  •       Biography      

    • I am the Research Director of the Research Engineering Group at the Qatar Computing Research Institute at Hamad Bin Khalifa University, Qatar Foundation. Before joining QCRI, I was a research associate professor at Purdue University. I have played a key role in establishing the data analytics group (now QCAI) within QCRI. I currently lead the Research Engineering Group whose mission is to productize QCRI’s research. My research interests lie in the fields of data management and analytics with a focus on data integration, data cleaning, and collaborative data science. I was the project lead of Rayyan, the leading systematic reviews web and mobile app, which is being used by more than 250k users worldwide. Rayyan has since graduated from QCRI to a start-up, Rayyan Systems Inc. I have been the Principal Investigator or Co-Principal Investigator in more than 15 grant proposals funded by NSF, NIH, DHS, and other funding agencies. I have received two Seeds of Success Awards from Purdue University. I hold a Ph.D. from Virginia Tech (1999-2004) and a B.Sc. and M.Sc. from the University of Science and Technology Houari Boumediene, Algiers, in Computer Science.
